Afterlife is an electronic music label , more specifically of the sub-genre called Melodic Tehno. Famous for their parties, Afterlife has sold out it's shows all around the world. It's line up has world class artists such as the italian duo and biggest star of the Melodic Techno scene, Tale Of Us formed by Matteo Milieri (ANYMA) and Carmine Conte (MRAK), the english duo, CamelPhat, the swiss duo, Adriatique amongst others.
