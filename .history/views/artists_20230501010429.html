TALE OF US

Tale of Us is an italian duo formed by Matteo Milleri and Carmine Conte. They are Afterlife's founders and nowdays melodich techno's most renowned artist. They played all arround the wolrd in some of the most well known festivals such as Tomorrowland (Belgium), Ultra (US) and Awakenings (Netherlands)

ANYMA

AYNMA is Matteo Milleri's (Tale of Us) solo alias. He started using it around May 2021 as an individual project in order to produce music alone and then use it in Tale of Us's shows. In November 2022 he introduced his solo visual show named 'GENESYS' wich includes most of his famous songs such as Etenity, The Sign, Explore Your Future, amongst others.

MRAK

MRAK is Carmine Conte's (Tale of Us) solo alias. As well as his friend ANYMA does, MRAK is Carmines's individual project in order to produce music alone and then use it in Tale of Us's shows. In November 2022 he introduced his solo show named 'WE DON'T FOLLOW wich includes most of his famous songs such as One, Vacio,Never Ends, amongst others.

ADRIATIQUE

ADRIATIQUE is a swiss duo Adrian Shala y Adrian Schweizer. The form part of AFTERLIFE since 2019. It's colaborations with artists from other genres such as The Weekend (Moth to a Flame - REMIX) made it's sound unique in the scene.

CAMELPHAT

CAMELPHAT in a british duo formed by Dave Whelan y Mike Di Scala. They jumped into the scene in 2004 and in 2017 released their famous track called "Cola". FIDELES and ANYMA made colaborations with them in "Night After Night - FIDELES - CAMELPHAT REMIX" and "The Sign".

COLYN

Ewout Colijn, best known for his alias "COLYN", was born in Netherlands where he started his DJing career. Nowdays he is one of the most famous djs in the wolrd with record breaking tracks such as "Oxygen levels low", "Unstable Gravity Alert" amongst others.

KEVIN DE VRIES

Kevin de Vries is a german dj who jumped into fame in 2022 with his most famous song "Dance With Me". Since then he's been in every AFTERLIFE line up being one of the most loved DJs by the crowd. With his german industrial sound he makes everyone dance all night long.

ØOSTIL

Øostil is an Argentinian DJ who jumped into fame in 2022 after an incredible performance as a warm up for Tale Of Us in Buenos Aires. He released some awesome tracks with world class DJs such as MASSANO in "Drown".
