TALE OF US

Tale of Us is an italian duo formed by Matteo Milleri and Carmine Conte. They are Afterlife's founders and nowdays melodich techno's most renowned artist. They played all arround the wolrd in some of the most well known festivals such as Tomorrowland (Belgium), Ultra (US) and Awakenings (Netherlands)

ANYMA
AYNMA is Matteo Milleri's (Tale of Us) solo alias. He started using it around May 2021 as an individual project in order to produce music alone and then use it in Tale of Us's shows. In November 2022 he introduced his solo visual show named GENESYS wich includes most of his famous songs such as Etenity, Night After Night, Explore Your Future, amongst others.
MRAK
MRAK is Carmine Conte's (Tale of Us) solo alias. As well as his friend AY as an individual project in order to produce music alone and then use it in Tale of Us's shows. In November 2022 he introduced his solo visual show named GENESYS wich includes most of his famous songs such as Etenity, Night After Night, Explore Your Future, amongst others.
